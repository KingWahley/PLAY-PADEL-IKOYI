SUPPORT CENTER

FIND ANSWERS TO COMMON QUESTIONS?

What should I wear?

Full athletic wear and non-marking indoor court shoes are mandatory. To maintain court quality, street shoes and denim are strictly prohibited in the arena.

How many people can play?

Padel is primarily a doubles sport (4 players). While you can play with 2 people for practice, our bookings cover up to 4 players per session.

Do I need experience?

Not at all! Padel is one of the world's fastest-growing sports because it's incredibly easy to learn. Our staff can provide a quick 5-minute brief for beginners.

Can children play?

Yes, Play Padel is a family-friendly environment. Children are welcome, though we require those under 12 to be supervised by an adult at all times.

Can I book coaching sessions?

Absolutely. We offer professional coaching for individuals or groups between 6:00 AM and 6:00 PM. You can book these through our "Booking" page.

Do I need to book in advance?

Yes. Due to high demand (especially during peak evening hours), we recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to secure your preferred slot.

Where are you located?

We are located in Ikoyi, Lagos.
