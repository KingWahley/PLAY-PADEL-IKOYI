ETIQUETTE

To maintain the prestige and performance levels of Elite Padel, we request all members and guests adhere to our structured club regulations.

COURT RULES

ADHERE TO TIME

Players must vacate the court exactly at the end of their session. Arrive 10 minutes prior to warm-up.

SPORTS ATTIRE

Full athletic wear and non-marking indoor court shoes are mandatory. No denim or street shoes permitted.

EQUIPMENT CARE

No equipment misuse. Rackets should not be struck against glass walls or net posts.

BOOKING RULES

  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance to receive credit.
  • Maximum of two peak-hour bookings per member per week.
  • Guests must be registered at the reception upon arrival.

SAFETY & CONDUCT

RESPECT PROTOCOL

Respect all players and staff. Foul language or aggressive behavior results in immediate suspension.

STAFF INSTRUCTIONS

Follow all instructions provided by court marshals and coaching staff without exception.

EMERGENCY EXIT

Keep court gates closed during play. Familiarize yourself with the emergency exits located at each wing.

HYDRATION

Glass containers are strictly prohibited on court. Use the provided hydration stations.
